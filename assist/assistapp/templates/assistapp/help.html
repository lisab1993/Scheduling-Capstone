Welcome to Assist!

The first thing you need to do to get started, is to add an event. Events are the primary job you need to do. It could be grocery shopping, spring cleaning, packing for a trip, or even planning a party! Each event will have tasks assigned to it.

Tasks are the smaller jobs that need to happen to accomplish your event. If your event is grocery shopping, your tasks might be the things you need to buy. You can set a date for when your task is due, set its urgency, and write as many notes as you'd like. To manage tasks for an event, click on the gold button that says "Tasks". You don't have to add any tasks at all if you don't want to, the event will still be on your calendar. There's no limit to the number of tasks you can have, so the sky is the limit!

The calendar button does exactly what you'd expect: it takes you to your calendar. The calendar will show all of your events along with their start times.

So what happens after an event has passed?
Old events go into the past events category. From the events page, click on the past events button to see them displayed in a list. You will have the same control over past events (and their tasks) as you do with new and upcoming events.
